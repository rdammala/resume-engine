## Role Summary
**High-level impact:** Define and scale reliability as a first-class capability at Grow Therapy, operating horizontally across the organization to shape how reliability is understood, measured, and built into developer experience.

## Key Responsibilities
1. **Define Reliability Standards** — Establish frameworks for SLOs/SLAs, error budgets, operational readiness; help teams understand what to measure and why
2. **Improve Observability & Measurement** — Identify gaps in metrics/logging/tracing; ensure services are measurable, debuggable, reliability-aligned
3. **Evolve Incident Response** — Develop/improve incident response (detection → post-incident learning); help teams build sustainable on-call/escalation patterns
4. **Enable Self-Service Reliability** — Partner with platform team to build tooling/abstractions (scorecards, dashboards, templates, golden paths) for adoption
5. **Drive Adoption Across Teams** — Educate, influence, guide engineering teams; scale reliability through standards, communication, developer-friendly systems
